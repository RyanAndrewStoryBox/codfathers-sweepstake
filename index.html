⚽ COUNTRY SWEEPSTAKE

6 seeds · 8 teams each · 1 per player · drawn worst to best

How it works: All 48 qualified nations are split across 6 seeds of 8, ranked by a blend of historical World Cup pedigree, current FIFA ranking, and genuine tournament threat — not the official FIFA draw pots, which were distorted by host nation privilege.
